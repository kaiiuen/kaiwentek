Quick tips

Balance your budget

Prioritize GPU and CPU spend for gaming builds, then add fast storage and enough memory to avoid bottlenecks.

Match sockets and chipsets

Motherboards and CPUs need compatible sockets. Check form factor support to ensure your case and board fit.

Plan for power headroom

Pick a PSU with at least 20% more wattage than your estimated draw so future upgrades stay stable.
